Privacy Policy - Subly

Last updated: January 2025
Extension: Subly - Intelligent Netflix Subtitles

1. Data Collection

Subly collects only the following data:

  • Netflix Subtitles: Subtitles from Netflix shows you watch (public data)
  • User Preferences: Your target language, native language, and vocabulary level settings (stored locally)

Subly does NOT collect:

  • Personal data (name, email, address)
  • Netflix account information
  • Browsing history
  • Location data
  • Payment information

2. Data Usage

Collected data is used exclusively for:

  • Subtitle Processing: Creating intelligent bilingual subtitles based on your vocabulary level
  • Personalization: Adapting the learning experience to your language preferences
  • Extension Functionality: Enabling automatic switching between target and native languages

3. Data Sharing

Subly does not share any data with third parties.

  • Subtitles are processed via our secure API and then deleted
  • Your preferences remain stored locally on your device
  • No data is transmitted to external services (except temporary subtitle processing)

4. Data Security

Security measures in place:

  • Secure Communication: All communications use HTTPS
  • Local Storage: Your preferences are stored only on your device
  • Temporary Processing: Subtitles are processed and deleted immediately
  • No Persistence: No data is retained on our servers

5. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data: Your preferences are visible in the extension interface
  • Modify your data: Change your settings at any time via the extension
  • Delete your data: Uninstall the extension to remove all local data
  • Control usage: Enable/disable the extension at any time

6. Cookies and Local Storage

Subly uses Chrome's local storage to save your preferences. No tracking cookies are used.

7. Regulatory Compliance

This privacy policy complies with GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) and Chrome Web Store requirements.

8. Policy Changes

We reserve the right to modify this privacy policy. Any changes will be communicated via an extension update.
